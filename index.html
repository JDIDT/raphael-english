Level 2

The Future Forge 🔮

📖 Two Ways to Talk About the Future

WILL + base verb

Spontaneous decisions, promises, predictions

  • ✦ I will help you build a house.
  • ✦ She will find diamonds.
  • ✦ It won't rain tomorrow.

GOING TO + base verb

Plans you already made, evidence-based predictions

  • ✦ I am going to explore the cave.
  • ✦ We are going to craft armour.
  • ✦ He isn't going to fight the dragon.

🇫🇷 "Je vais explorer" ≈ "I am going to explore" — same structure!
